Mr & Mrs
Burkhardt

Nick fell in love with Dr. Juliette Silverton when he was investigating a hit-and-run case. In 2009 he invited her to move in with him. Now, they want to get married as soon as possible.

Nick
Burkhardt

Nicholas "Nick" Burkhardt (born June 18, 1982) is a Grimm and detective for the Portland Police Department. Growing up, he was not exposed to any information regarding his Grimm heritage or the Wesen that surrounded him. He learned about these largely from his own experiences since 2011, which made him unique among Grimms and generally promoting the development of more socially acceptable interaction with all individuals.

Juliette
Silverton

Doctor Juliette Silverton is a domestic animal veterinarian. She studied at Oregon State University, located about 90 minutes from her and Nick's former home in Portland, Oregon. She worked at the Roseway Veterinary Hospital beginning in 2006.
